What is Webicity?

Webicity is a HTML-based web browser for the ComputerCraft mod



Why does it matter?

Previously CC-based web, internet, and rednet browsers would use their own markup languages. This means that you could only go to specific sites, and other sites would not work. Webicity is an easy-to-use web & filesystem browser with primary support for HTML, meaning you can browse billions of pages and download millions of files - unrestricted
